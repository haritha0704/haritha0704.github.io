Neighborhood Map

This application is an interactive map of Omaha NE with locations(resturants) that I am interested in marked on the map. When one of the locations are clicked on a popup window will display additional information about that resturants including their rating from Foursquare.

There is also a completely integrated instant search feature to allow the user to search through the locations marked on the map.
